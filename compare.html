"Expected profit" is the average over 20,000 simulated portfolios where each card gems at its PSA-population rate (adjusted down for small samples). Gem rates come from PSA's own published population data — and published rates run optimistic, because people pre-screen before grading. The allocator has sliders to stress every assumption on this page.
